Background

Climate change demands urgent action ...

  • Rapid adoption of green technologies (IPCC, 2023)

  • Large-scale transformation is required (Lema and Perez, 2024)

  • Foster a new techno-economic paradigm (Hall, 2006)

Techno-economic paradigms

(Perez, 2003; Freeman and Louçã 2001)

  • Evolve naturally and slowly

  • Reshape productive processes, routines and institutions  

  • Predictable technological progress

Motivation

  • Unpredictable green returns (Polzin, 2017)

  • Negative uncertainty-investment relation (Caballero, 1991)

  • Vicious circle: low diffusion < > low investments

Financial implications

At the same time ...

  • Supports decarbonization (Campiglio and Van der Ploeg, 2022)

  • Absorbs technological risks (D’Orazio and Valente 2019)

  • Speeds up the transition (Mazzucato and Penna, 2015)
